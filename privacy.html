FewStep Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

("we," "us," or "our") operates the FewStep mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

FewStep collects limited data to provide its core functionality. The primary reason we store your data is to **ensure you can sync your habits, routines, and progress across any device** using your account credentials. We may collect and store the following information:

  • User-created habits, routines, and tasks
  • Progress and activity data
  • App usage data

This data is securely stored using cloud services (Firebase) to enable synchronization and app functionality. We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial data, or government IDs.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure data storage, database management, and app functionality.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for ads and analytics.

Privacy policies: Firebase & Google Privacy Policy

3. Log Data

In case of app errors, we may collect Log Data such as device IP address, device name, OS version, app configuration, and usage timestamps. This is used for debugging and improving performance.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices via Firebase. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

FewStep does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, we will delete it promptly.

6. User Consent

By using FewStep, you agree to the collection and use of information as described.
